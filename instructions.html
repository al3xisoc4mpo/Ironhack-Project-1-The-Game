How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move the spaceship in all four directions (up,down,left,right).

Use the space key to shoot proyectiles but be careful! Use them wisely as they deplete the fuel reserves

Hitting and asteroid will lower your shields. Blast an asteroid with a proyectile!

Enter in contact with a black hole and loose a great amount of fuel due the gravitational pull. Stay away from them!

Keep an eye on your shield, if you are hit by an asteroid with zero shields you loose
Fuel is consumed constantly; so monitor you fuel-reserves. Ran out of it and you loose.

You win when distance to home is zero!
